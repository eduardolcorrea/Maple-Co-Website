About Us

Maple & Co. was founded in 2018 by lifelong coffee lovers Emma and James Maple. What started as a dream became Portland's most beloved neighborhood coffee shop.

We source our beans ethically from small farms across Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala. Every cup tells a story.
